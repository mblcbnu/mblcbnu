Project Overview

This project focuses on establishing a comprehensive End-to-End Whole-Genome Sequencing (WGS) integrated platform for six major types of animal-derived Antimicrobial-Resistant Bacteria (ARB). The platform encompasses the entire lifecycle from sequencing to functional genomics, mobility prediction, risk assessment, and interactive visualization. Led by Prof. Tatsuya Unno (Chungbuk National University), this 2-year research initiative is supported by the Animal and Plant Quarantine Agency (APQA), with the research period spanning from January 2026 to November 2027.

01. Standardized SOPs

Establishing standardized protocols for pre-processing, assembly, and QC by comparing Illumina, ONT, and Hybrid sequencing data performance.

02. Precision Pipelines

Automated analysis of functional elements (ARG, VF, Promoter, RBS) and ML-based prediction of AST phenotypes and gene transferability.

03. Risk Assessment UI

Calculating integrated risk indices based on ARG hazard and MGE burden, provided through a researcher-ready web platform.
